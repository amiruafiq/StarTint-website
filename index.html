Tentang Kami

Star Tint is a professional window tinting company based in Marang, Terengganu, dedicated to providing high-quality tint film solutions for residential and commercial spaces. Serving clients across Kuala Terengganu, Marang, and nearby regions, we specialise in the installation of premium window films designed to reduce heat, block harmful UV rays, and enhance indoor comfort and privacy.

We are committed to using quality-assured materials and modern techniques to ensure lasting results that meet the expectations of today’s homeowners and businesses. At Star Tint, our goal is to create cooler, safer, and more energy-efficient environments — one window at a time.
